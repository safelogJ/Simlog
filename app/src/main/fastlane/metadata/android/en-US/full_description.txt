An app for recording SIM card signal strength and data transfer (internet) history. The app saves received data (minute by minute) and creates a daily graph.
📡 Supported router models: Currently tested with the following devices:
🔹 Mikrotik — all models with an LTE interface, use the script
🔹 Keenetic (Netcraze) — models with LTE and USB modems
🔹 Cudy — LT500 V2.0
🔹 TP-Link — TL-MR150 v2
🔹 Huawei — CPE B593s-22
🔹 Huawei — E3372h-320
